What is Nyquil Chicken

Nyquil Chicken is well.. Chicken cooked in good ol' Nyquil. As you can imagine, it is highly nutritious and is the Ohio BOSS equivalent to the Cold and Flu. Why need a vaccine when you can have Nyquil Chicken

The Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • NyQuil (Preferably 3 Years past expiry)
  • Salt (RbCl)

Here are the steps:

  1. First, pour 15 liters of NyQuil into a hot pan
  2. When the NyQuil starts to boil, throw your chicken breasts in the pan at a velocity of 800 m/s
  3. Let the chicken cook well for 6 Hours, stirring occasionally
  4. After 6 hours, take out the chicken from the pan with a pitchfork and garnish with Rubidium Chloride
  5. Dig In!

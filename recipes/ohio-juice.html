Ohio-Juice - Odin's

What is Ohio

Ohio Juice is a simple, tasty and replenishing drink that'll keep you hydrated until you die! Ohio Juice was created by Gay Bowser, renowned food artist of 23rd century.

The Ingredients

  • Water extracted from abandoned sewers
  • Unstable Plutonium extract (Preferably before half-life)
  • Shredded Goat Excrements (RbCl)
  • Lithium-Ion electrolyte Juice

Here are the steps:

  1. First, add 600 ml of sewer water into a lead glass
  2. Second, add 200 ml of Li-Ion electrolyte Juice and stir well
  3. Add the Shredded Goat Excrements generously into the cup
  4. Finally, add 3 ml of unstable plutonium
  5. Put the drink into an exposed microwave for 6 seconds
  6. Bottoms Up!
